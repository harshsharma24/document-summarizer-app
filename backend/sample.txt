Artificial intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on building systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include speech recognition, decision-making, language translation, and more.

Recent advancements in machine learning and deep learning have led to significant improvements in AI capabilities. Companies across industries are leveraging AI for automation, predictive analytics, and personalized recommendations.

AI continues to evolve, and its applications are expanding rapidly in fields like healthcare, finance, and autonomous vehicles.
